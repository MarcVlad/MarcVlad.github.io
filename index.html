About

Let me introduce myself.

I am the director of the NGO A.M.OR which aims to teach digital skills for children in need. Leading an international team, I want to give back to the world especially to the youth. I am an advocator for privacy rules in our digital society and love sharing ways of protecting one's digital exhaust. I am also promoting OpenSource / OpenAccess content in any projects I am part of as it allows a complete understanding of the digital tool in use and, reduce the digital gap by sharing resources freely. Since I can recall, I am passionate about Geography and more specificly how the global energy landscape and its political, economic and environmental tenets draw our relation to earth. The new way of producing, consuming and transmitting energy fascinates me and I believe there are great investment opportunities yet to explore. In the future, I wish to keep working in such meaningful sectors and want to work in conflict zones and provide all my expertise in energy system, teaching and humanitarian project management to people affected by conflicts.
