About

Let me introduce myself

I am an Innovation Officer with a Master’s in Environmental Sciences (major in Energy studies) from the University of Geneva. With a growing passion for new technologies and new business models, I am an advocator for privacy rules in our digital society and love sharing ways of protecting one's digital exhaust. I am passionate about open-source processes, and I have deep knowledge of the blockchain industry, focusing on decentralized/public and interoperable protocols. I am particularly interested in Decentralized Governance, Finance, AI and Data, and I am engaged in various DAOs that enable community building and advocate for data portability as well as equal access to financial services and technologies. I believe crypto is the most important OpenSource initative of our century and allow a complete understanding of the digital tool in use and, reduce the gap of digital litteracy and financial servicess by sharing resources freely. Since I can recall, I am passionate about Geography and more specificly about how the global energy landscape and its political, economic and environmental tenets draw our relation to earth. The new way of producing, consuming and transmitting energy and data fascinates me and I believe there are great investment opportunities yet to explore. In the future, I wish to keep working in such meaningful sectors to provide all my expertise in data portability, energy systems, teaching and project management.
